What services does ABC - Apparel Branding Company offer?

ABC specializes in custom apparel branding solutions including screen printing, embroidery, heat transfer, and digital printing. We also offer design services, bulk ordering, and corporate branding packages tailored to your needs.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Our minimum order quantities vary by service: Screen printing starts at 24 pieces, embroidery at 12 pieces, and heat transfer at 6 pieces. For digital printing, we can accommodate orders as small as 1 piece. Contact us for specific requirements and bulk pricing.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Standard turnaround time is 7-10 business days after design approval. Rush orders can be completed in 3-5 business days for an additional fee. Large orders may require additional time. We'll provide specific timing estimates with your quote.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, our in-house design team can help create custom designs for your apparel. We offer complimentary basic design consultation and can develop complete custom designs for a fee. We can also work with your existing artwork or logos.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and corporate purchase orders. For new clients, we require a 50% deposit to begin production, with the remaining balance due before shipping. Regular clients may qualify for net-30 terms.

Do you offer samples before full production?

Yes, we can provide pre-production samples for approval. Sample costs vary based on the printing method and quantity needed. Sample fees are typically credited toward your final order when you proceed with full production.
